About Me

Background

I'm a Data Scientist with over 12 years of experience spanning computer vision, deep learning, IoT analytics, and enterprise data engineering. Currently at SICK Sensor Intelligence in Hamburg, Germany, I build scalable perception and analytics systems for autonomous and safety-critical applications.

My work ranges from designing ROS2-based edge perception stacks deployed on NVIDIA Jetson hardware to architecting end-to-end cloud data pipelines on AWS and Azure. I've trained and deployed models like YOLOX, RTMDet, and PointPillars for real-world object detection and tracking tasks.

Previously at Tata Consultancy Services, I led a 15-member team delivering data-warehousing, BI, and risk-analytics solutions for global banking clients including Citi Bank.
